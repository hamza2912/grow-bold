Experience

Education
Transformation Kenya

The Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development (KICD) had a vision to promote and create 21st century student centered learning environments in Kenyan schools which would allow every learner in Kenya to access quality, relevant, personalized and adaptive learning anytime and anywhere. KICD has over the last 10 years invested in digital content to support curriculum delivery through the use of Technology and the Government of Kenya embarked on a Digital Literacy Project (DLP) that has seen the deployment of 2 million student and teacher computers in Kenyan schools.
